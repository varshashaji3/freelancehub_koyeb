Frequently Asked Questions

What is a freelance services platform?

A freelance services platform connects freelancers with clients who need various services, such as graphic design, writing, programming, and more. It allows freelancers to showcase their skills and clients to find the right talent for their projects.

How do I post a job?

To post a job, simply create an account, navigate to the 'Post a Job' section, and fill in the details of your project. Make sure to provide clear and concise information to attract the right freelancers.

How can I manage proposals from freelancers?

You can manage proposals from freelancers by going to your job posting page. Here, you will see all the proposals submitted for your job. You can review, communicate, and select the best proposal that fits your needs.

How are payments handled?

Payments are handled securely through our platform. Clients deposit funds into an escrow account, and the payment is released to the freelancer once the project is completed and approved. This ensures security for both parties.

What support is available if I encounter issues?

We offer 24/7 support to assist you with any issues or questions you may have. You can contact our support team through the 'Contact Us' page, and we will respond promptly to help resolve your concerns.

How can I track the progress of my project?

You can track the progress of your project using our progress tracking tools. Freelancers can update their status, submit milestones, and communicate with you regularly to ensure that the project stays on track.

What happens if there is a dispute?

In case of a dispute, our platform offers a dispute resolution service. Both parties can submit their case, and our team will review the information provided and work to mediate a fair resolution.

How do I leave a review for a freelancer?

After your project is completed, you can leave a review for the freelancer on their profile. This helps other clients know about the freelancer's performance and reliability, and it helps freelancers build their reputation.
